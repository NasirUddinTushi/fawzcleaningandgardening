About Fawz Cleaning & Gardening

Founded with purpose and passion, Fawz Cleaning & Gardening began trading in May 2025, and we bring with us hands-on experience dating back to 2024. Based in the Northern Territory, we are proud to serve domestic, commercial, industrial, hospitality and education-sector clients across the region.

What we do

We specialise in:

  • Bond / End-of-lease cleaning
  • Spring cleaning
  • Gardening and grounds maintenance
  • Lawn mowing and garden care
  • House maintenance services
  • Mailbox replacement and general handyman jobs

Whether you’re a homeowner, property-manager, business or institution, we tailor our services to match your needs and site-type.

Our Promise

At Fawz, our goal is simple: guaranteed work, high customer satisfaction, and minimal price. We understand that your time, space and budget matter — so we aim to deliver professional, insured, reliable results at a fair price. Working with property management companies and cleaning firms already, we know what it takes to meet high standards and tight turnaround times.

Why Choose Us

  • Experienced in the Northern Territory environment, with local knowledge and care.
  • Serving a wide range of clients: domestic, commercial, industrial, hospitality and education.
  • Full-service: cleaning, gardening, maintenance and handyman tasks under one roof.
  • Commitment to customer satisfaction and delivering on our guarantee.

We look forward to helping you keep your property looking its best.
